Privacy and Cookie Policy

How Rockport Uses and Protects Your Data

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

This privacy policy explains why, when and how we may collect personal information about visitors to our website.

Why do we collect Personal information about you?

We collect personal information in order to:

  • Improve our understanding of how visitors use this website.

When do we collect personal information?

  • When you visit our website and consent to analytics cookies, we collect anonymised information about your IP address, geographical location, browser type and which pages you have visited via Google Analytics. This information is only collected if you accept analytics cookies.
  • When you send us email correspondence, we will store any personal information contained within the email.

How do we use your information?

The data we collect is used as follows:

  • To contact you in response to enquiries.
  • To improve the browsing experience for our website visitors by analysing how people access our website; how people find the website, from which browsers and operating systems people use to access the website and from which countries people use our website.

We do not use personal information for marketing or promotional purposes without your written permission.



Cookie Policy

Your consent

We only set analytics cookies with your explicit consent. When you first visit this website, a banner will ask whether you accept analytics cookies. If you decline, no analytics cookies will be set. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site, or by contacting us.

What cookies do we use?

This website uses the following cookies:

Cookie name Provider Purpose Type Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated identifier. Used to calculate visitor, session and campaign data. Analytics — only set with consent 2 years
_ga_G-8XFFYQRQDJ Google Analytics Maintains session state for this specific GA4 property. Analytics — only set with consent 2 years

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to analyse how visitors use this website, including which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and how they found the site. IP addresses are anonymised before being processed by Google. Google stores aggregated statistical data and provides summary reports to us. We do not have access to individual visitor data. Analytics data is retained in our Google Analytics account for 14 months. For more information, see the Google Analytics privacy policy and Google privacy policy.

YouTube video embeds

Some pages on this website include embedded YouTube videos hosted on youtube-nocookie.com. This domain is YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode, which does not set cookies unless you click to play a video. If you play a video, YouTube may set cookies on your device subject to YouTube's own cookie policy. We have no control over these cookies.

Website hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., a subsidiary of Microsoft). GitHub may process your IP address as part of serving website content. See the GitHub privacy statement for details.

Managing cookies in your browser

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable analytics cookies, you will still be able to use this website normally. Note that browser-level blocking does not remove the consent preference cookie (rs_analytics_consent), which is a functional cookie stored on your device.

Personal Information

Who has access to your information?

  • We do not sell, rent or share your information with any third parties.
  • We may disclose personal information to our employees as deemed reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy.

How do we protect your information?

  • We take great care to treat your information securely. We have in place procedures to try to prevent unauthorised access. We use technical precautions to prevent the loss or misuse of your personal information.
  • We store all personal information on our secure servers, which are protected by passwords and firewalls.

Personal information may also be stored temporarily on servers hosted by the following services.

  • Microsoft 365. This is our email provider. Follow these links (external sites) to view the Microsoft privacy policy. Email is sent to us over the Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP). Our SMTP servers are protected by TLS meaning that the email content is encrypted before being sent across the internet. The email content is then decrypted by our local computers and devices.
  • GitHub Pages. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub Inc. (a Microsoft subsidiary). GitHub may temporarily process visitor IP addresses as part of serving web content. Follow this link to view the GitHub privacy statement.

Links to other websites

Our website contains links to websites run by other organisations. This privacy policy only applies to our website (please read the privacy statements on the other websites).

Is your personal information transferred outside the UK or the EEA?

We are based in the UK but sometimes your personal information may be transferred outside the European Economic Area. If we do so we will make sure that suitable safeguards are in place, for example by using approved contractual agreements, unless certain exceptions apply.

Data Breaches

We will report any personal information data breaches to the relevant persons and authorities within 72 hours of the breach if it is apparent that personal data stored in an identifiable manner has been stolen.
