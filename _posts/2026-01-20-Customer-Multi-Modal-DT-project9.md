description: Rockport uses an innovative digital twin-to-digital twin interaction model as the basis for simulations. By using an existing airport digital twins, our simulation platform can leverage existing data feed investments to instantiate the baseline simulation of the airport. Using synthetic atomic events, the simulator generates realistic scenarios to test and optimise the virtual airport. The atomic events define the attributes of physical entities to dynamically compose a 3D visualisation of the airport. Our simulation scenario platform allows facilities, assets, services and processes to be simulated based on their known state, capacities and behaviours.
